Governance

Governance Structure

Board of Directors

A group of 7-10 individuals with expertise in cybersecurity, governance, and non-profit management

Executive Committee

A subset of the Board of Directors, responsible for overseeing daily operations and making strategic decisions.

Advisory Council

A group of experts in cybersecurity, academia, and industry, providing guidance and recommendations to the board of Directors

Roles

Board of Directors Roles

  • Chairperson: Presides over Board meetings and ensures effective governance
  • Vice-Chairperson: Supports the Chairperson and assumes responsibilities in their absence
  • Treasurer: oversees financial management and reporting
  • Secretary: Responsible for record-keeping and communication.

Executive Committee Roles

  • Executive Director: Leads daily operations and implements strategic decisions.
  • Deputy Director: Supports the Executive Director and assumes responsibilities in their absence.

Advisory Council Roles

  • Cybersecurity Experts: Provide guidance on technical matters and emerging threats.
  • Academic Representatives: Offer insights on research and development in cybersecurity.
  • Industry Partners: Share expertise on market trends and industry best practices.
